Our Story

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Gaborone, Harvest Best was born from a passion for preserving Botswana's culinary heritage while embracing modern dining experiences.

Our founder, Chef Tumi Moremi, envisioned a space where traditional Setswana recipes could be celebrated with contemporary flair, using only the freshest local ingredients.

Our Philosophy

  • Source 90% of ingredients within Botswana
  • Preserve traditional cooking methods
  • Support local farmers and producers
  • Train and employ local talent
